I text a lot

Using the text logs from six months of group chat, I decided to visualize how a close group of my friends communicates on WhatsApp. The results? I talk too much, sometimes we get really hypochondriac and we chat a lot during work hours. Shame on us.


No respect for work hours

Think of this chart as a clock. A weird non-circular 24-hour clock, but a clock nevertheless. The longer the pie slice, the more messages were sent in that hour of the day. This shows that my friends only really start texting in the late morning. Chat gets really intense on lunch, but lasts only until we get to work, around 12 PM... Nevertheless, after a while the boredom of newsroom life strikes and we text and text and text and text until we sleep. Pretty exciting.
